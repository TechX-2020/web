Mandarmani

Destination

Suncity Resort, Mandarmani

"High achievement always take place in the framework of high expectation."
Any event can be great by the ambience of the place where it happens. A place sets the environment as well as the mood of the participants. Without further ado we bring you the venue for the Tech-X Congress. ‘Suncity Resort Mandarmani’. Mandarmani is a seaside resort village (largest) in the state of West Bengal, India, and lies in East Midnapore district, at the northern end of the Bay of Bengal. With the breezes that come with the sea currents, the beautiful beaches and the everlasting sea will give you the journey full of inspiration, learning and fun. As it said that the best lessons learned are those that are taught outside the four walls of classroom so here we bring you a whole new beautiful classroom where you can enjoy learning, experience a whole new adventure and know many people from all around the country. So join us in this learning vacation revolving around amazing sunset view, steamy bonfire, placid strolling along the beach, provoking keynote speeches, captivating workshops and much more!
